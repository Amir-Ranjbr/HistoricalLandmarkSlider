The Colosseum

The Colosseum is a huge amphitheater in Rome, Italy. A very long time ago, the ancient Romans built it for exciting shows and fights, especially gladiator contests. The Colosseum which is made of strong materials like concrete and sand is a symbol of ancient Roman engineering and entertainment.

The Pyramids

The Pyramids are giant structures in Egypt made a long time ago. They were built as fancy tombs for important kings called pharaohs. The Great Pyramid of Giza is the most famous one and is really special. People used big stones to make these pyramids, and they are a symbol of ancient Egypt.

Machu Picch

Machu Picchu was built around the 15th century in the mountains of Peru. This remarkable site is renowned for its breathtaking mountainous setting and intricate stone structures. It is designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and it stands as one of the most important archaeological wonders, offering insights into the advanced civilization of the Incas.
